Short Bio

Hi — I'm Zixuan Zhang, an LLM developer at Amazon, working on post-training, RL, and agentic capabilities for Rufus foundation models at Amazon.

I am a major contributor to the post-training and RL recipe design of Rufus model releases from 2024 to 2026, covering both in-house pre-trained and open-source base models, MoE and dense architectures, and small, mid, and large-scale models, achieving state-of-the-art on mathematical/STEM reasoning and agentic/terminal coding. My work mainly includes synthetic data generation, RL recipe development, and environment design and scaling across reasoning, coding, and product-specific agentic applications in shopping scenarios.

My research mainly lies in building Knowledge-enhanced Language Models. Recently, I am particularly interested in making the knowledge enhancements to LLMs more generalizable, by enabling automatic knowledge extraction, disentangled knowledge storage, and consistent knowledge updates. I have also worked on other fundamental NLP tasks like information extraction, knowledge graph completion, question answering, and text summarization.
